Job description

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Be responsible for requirement analysis and identification of solution, system testing and ownership till deployment.
  • Develop deep understanding of the application ecosystem and responsible for impact analysis, problem solving and performance optimization of the code.
  • Identify and proactively come up with solutions to address complex issues and enhance scalability of the systems
  • Ownership of multiple module delivery while steering and breaking problems into smaller modules with an understanding constraints to arrive at the optimal solution.
  • Collaborate with multiple stakeholders to deliver products in an agile environment.
  • Responsible for peer and junior level review of code and ensure sharing of best practices within the team and on various platforms.


Desired Candidate Profile

  • Ability to drive design and architecture of multiple subsystems.
  • Able to break complex projects into modules and propose effective solutions in view of capabilities of existing platforms and infrastructure.
  • Expertise in Java microservices, REST APIs, Mysql, Caching technologies like Aerospace, Queuing technologies like Kafka, AW
  • Understanding of Agile methodologies
